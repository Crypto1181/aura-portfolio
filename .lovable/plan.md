## Design System
- **Background**: Pure white (#FFFFFF) for main, black for nav/sections
- **Accent**: Yellow (#FFD600) for badges, prices, CTA section
- **Red**: (#FF3300) for labels like "SELECTED_WORKS"
- **Typography**: Bold, uppercase, tight letter-spacing, industrial/brutalist feel
- **Monospace** font for subtitles and tech stack labels
- **Cards**: Large images with overlaid text, no rounded corners (sharp brutalist edges)
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover scale on cards, smooth transitions
